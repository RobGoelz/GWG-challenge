Tanuki

Tanuki are a recurring theme in Japanese popular culture.

  • Scientific Name: Nyctereutes procyonoides viverrinus
  • Average Length: 45 to 71 cm
  • Average Lifespan: 6 to 7 years
  • Habitat: East Asia

The Japanese raccoon dog, also known as tanuki (狸 or たぬき, [taꜜnɯki]) in Japanese, is a subspecies of the Asian raccoon dog.
